Get in Touch

If you're interested in lessons, please read over the information below then send me a message. Let me know what you're interested in and tell me about your background in music, if any.

Beginning Students

Do you need a conservatory-trained teacher if you're a just a beginner? That's up to you! I will say that I specialize in building strong technical and practice foundations right from the start, something not all teachers can offer. I've also developed a systematic way of encorporating music theory into lessons, either in examining current repertoire, or as exercises played at the keyboard.

Trial Lessons

You can try one lesson first for $30. This will give you an idea of what lessons will be like. I can provide advice on getting an instrument if you don't have one already.

General Requirements

You will need to practice daily.

Piano Student Requirements

You will need to have a keyboard with fully weighted keys or an acoustic piano at home, or have regular access to one.

Guitar Student Requirements

I only take beginning guitar students.

You will need an acoustic guitar, with a case so you can bring it to lessons, and a music stand. I highly recommend a classical guitar to start even if you're interested in popular styles: a classical guitar's nylon strings are much easier on beginners' fingers.
