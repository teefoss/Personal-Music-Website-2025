<p>
Ezra Fynn is an American composer and pianist. Primarily a composer for piano,
his music takes influence from post-minimalism and ambient styles, expressing
depth of emotion though simplicity.
</p>

<p>
Ezra is classicaly trained, but now works in a contemporary style. He studied
composition and performance in New York and later performance at the
<a href="https://rcm.ac.uk">Royal College of Music</a> in London.
</p>

<p>
Ezra lives in Cambridge, Massachusetts, where he spends his time composing,
teaching, and meditating.
</p>
