Teaching

I teach piano, composition, music theory, and improvisation to adult students on Chauncy Street in Cambridge.

New Students

See New Student Information.

Communication

For general communication and booking, please email me. For day of the lesson communication, like cancellations, please text.

Booking Lessons

Confirm lesson time(s) in person or by email, then make a payment to book it.

Please be sure you'll be available at the time(s) you book, payments are non-refundable.

Rescheduling

A reasonable amount of rescheduling is allowed, but is dependent on my availability. For illness, let me know as soon as possible. For all other reasons, please give 24 hours notice. Lessons cancelled with less than 24 hours notice are not eligable to be rescheduled.

Arriving

Use the main entrance—through the arch, down the path. The intercom doesn't work, so text when you arrive. If the entrance is unlocked, you can come in and knock (loudly!) on my door at your lesson time even if you hear music or a lesson in progress.

Payment

Please pay in advance with cash or Venmo. Your payment serves as a booking confirmation and holds the lesson time(s). A lower rate applies when paying for 4 or more lessons in a month.

Rates

Piano lessons start at $240 per month. Guitar lessons start at $220 per month. Paying for individual lessons is also possible.
